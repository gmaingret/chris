# Plan 46-02 — L10N-01 `/profile` psychological-section localization

## Objective

Localize the 21 EN-only sites in `src/bot/handlers/profile.ts` that reach Greg's FR/RU `/profile` output: HEXACO/Schwartz dim labels (12 strings), psychological-section qualifier function (3 strings — folds in L10N-05 import), and the per-dim score-line template tokens `"/ 5.0"` + `"confidence"` + `"—"` separator (6 instances of the template via the two `lines.push` calls). Adds FR + RU golden snapshots to `profile-psychological.golden.test.ts` filling the Scenario 4 hook stubbed by Phase 39 D-25. Closes L10N-01.

## Phase Boundary

In scope:
- Replace `qualifierForPsych` local function (`src/bot/handlers/profile.ts:704-708`) with `qualifierFor` imported from `src/chris/locale/strings.ts` (Plan 46-01's module). Update both call sites at `:791` + `:806` to pass `lang`.
- Convert `HEXACO_DIM_DISPLAY_LABELS` (`:714-721`) and `SCHWARTZ_DIM_DISPLAY_LABELS` (`:727-738`) from `Readonly<Record<key, string>>` to `Readonly<Record<key, Record<Lang, string>>>` shape. Look up `[key][lang]` at use sites.
- Extend `MSG` register with a `scoreLine` template per Lang that produces the per-dim line including `/ 5.0` and `confidence` tokens.
- Update the two `lines.push` template-string sites at `:790-792` + `:805-807` to call `MSG.scoreLine[lang](label, score, conf, qualifierResult)`.
- Extend `profile-psychological.golden.test.ts` Scenario 1 + Scenario 3 with FR + RU snapshot variants (passing `'French'` / `'Russian'` to the formatter).

Out of scope:
- Operational-section labels (`MSG.sectionTitle.jurisdictional` etc.) — already localized in Phase 35/39.
- Prompt-side dim labels in `src/memory/profiles.ts` — stay EN per CONTEXT.md Deferred section.
- Number-format locale (FR-FR `4,2` comma-decimal vs `4.2` dot-decimal) — covered by `Number.prototype.toFixed` which always emits dot; per 29-REVIEW WR-03 the comma-decimal expectation is acknowledged but deferred (not in REQUIREMENTS.md L10N-01 wording). Score values stay `dim.score.toFixed(1)` with dot.
- The `WEEKLY_REVIEW_HEADER`, `TEMPLATED_FALLBACK`, journal PROMPTS — those belong to Plans 46-03 + 46-04.
