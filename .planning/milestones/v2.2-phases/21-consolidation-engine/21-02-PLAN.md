<!-- Shape (not the exact prose — executor composes the final text). The assembled prompt flows in this order: -->

1. `CONSTITUTIONAL_PREAMBLE` (imported verbatim from personality.ts — CONS-04)

2. Role preamble: "You are generating an episodic summary of Greg's day. Describe what actually happened. Do not soften negative experiences, reframe frustration as growth, or characterize indecision as wisdom. Preserve the emotional register of the raw entries as expressed."

3. Importance rubric (CONS-05):
   - Anchors: "Score 1–3: mundane, routine, no emotional intensity. Score 4–6: notable — at least one decision, strong emotion, or meaningful event. Score 7–9: significant — structural decision, contradiction surfaced, sustained emotional intensity, or novel context. Score 10: life-event-rare — once-a-year magnitude."
   - Frequency guidance: "Most days are 3–6. Scores of 7+ should be uncommon across a sustained period (roughly 10–20% of days). Score 10 should be rare — fewer than 5% of days in a year."
   - Chain-of-thought instruction: "Before assigning the score, explicitly reason through: (a) emotional intensity, (b) novelty vs. routine, (c) decision presence (captured or resolved), (d) contradiction presence."
   - Do NOT anchor to the midpoint. Err toward the extremes when the evidence supports it.

4. Verbatim quote enforcement (CONS-10): "Each entry in key_quotes must be a verbatim substring of an entry from the day. Do not paraphrase, summarize, or clean up grammar. The quote must be findable by exact substring match in the source Pensieve entry text."

5. Contradiction preservation block (CONS-09) — included ONLY when `input.contradictions.length > 0`:
   "The following pairs of statements from today were flagged as contradictions (M002 confidence >= 0.75). Preserve both positions verbatim. Do not smooth them into a single resolved arc. Use the format: 'Greg held contradictory positions on [X]: [Y] versus [Z]. These were not reconciled.' Contradictions flagged today: [list of {A, B, description} triples, verbatim]."

6. Decision context block (CONS-06/CONS-07 prompt-side enforcement + CONS-08 data injection) — included ONLY when `input.decisions.length > 0`:
   "The following decisions were created or resolved today (M007). For each, the lifecycle_state reflects the state as of this date — do NOT describe a decision in state 'open' or 'due' as 'decided' or 'resolved' in the summary unless the entries explicitly contain a resolution statement and lifecycle_state is 'resolved' or 'reviewed'.

   Decisions:
   - [decisionText] (state: [lifecycleState])
     Reasoning: [reasoning]
     Forecast: [prediction]
     Falsification criterion: [falsificationCriterion]
     {if resolution != null:} Resolution: [resolution]
     {if resolutionNotes != null:} Notes: [resolutionNotes]"

   Additionally, when any decision was captured/resolved today, append the floor hook instruction: "A structural decision was captured or resolved today — importance score MUST be at least 6." (CONS-06)

   When any contradiction was flagged today, append: "A contradiction was flagged today — importance score MUST be at least 7." (CONS-07)

   These are prompt-level instructions; Plan 21-04's runConsolidate MAY additionally clamp the Zod-parsed output's `importance` to enforce the floor as a belt-and-suspenders (executor's decision — recommended, but prompt-level enforcement is the requirement).

7. Sparse-entry guard (CONS-11) — included ONLY when sparse mode triggers (entries < 3 OR total content tokens < 100, approximated by character count / 4):
   "This day has very few entries. You may only state what is explicitly present in the source entries. Do not infer, speculate, or elaborate beyond what Greg wrote. When an entry is sparse, the summary for that entry must be sparse. Write 'Greg noted: [what he wrote]' rather than expanding on what he might have meant. The `topics` array may contain only topics directly present in the entries. The `key_quotes` array may be empty if no direct quotes meet the verbatim criterion."

8. Entries block: "Today's Pensieve entries (verbatim, timestamped in config.proactiveTimezone):
   [each entry rendered as: '- [HH:MM, source] content\n']"

9. Structured-output directive: "Return your answer as a single JSON object matching the provided schema. Fields: summary (prose narrative, minimum 50 chars), importance (integer 1–10), topics (1–10 free-form topic tags), emotional_arc (one-sentence description of the day's emotional trajectory), key_quotes (0–10 verbatim substrings from the entries)."
